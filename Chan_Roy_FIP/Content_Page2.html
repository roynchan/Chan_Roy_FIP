Tim Hortons

Tim Hortons is a Canadian coffee chain restaurant, The company was founded in 1964 in Hamilton, Ontario. And became a Canada-favourite coffee shop, ever since you stepped on the street in London you’ll see people holding a cup of Coffee from Tim Hortons. Tim Hortons sell different kind of coffee and snacks, their most famous product is their classic Double-Double.

Starbucks

Starbucks is a world wide international coffee shop, it has over 83 market all over the world , one of the most well-known coffee shop around the world, Starbucks was founded in Seattle, Washington in 1971.
