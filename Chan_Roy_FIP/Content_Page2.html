Tim Hortons

Tim Hortons is a Canadian coffee chain restaurant, The company was founded in 1964 in Hamilton, Ontario. And became a Canada-favourite coffee shop, ever since you stepped on the street in London you’ll see people holding a cup of Coffee from Tim Hortons. Tim Hortons sell different kind of coffee and snacks, their most famous product is their classic Double-Double.

Starbucks

Starbucks is a world wide international coffee shop, it has over 83 market all over the world , one of the most well-known coffee shop around the world, Starbucks was founded in Seattle, Washington in 1971. As of 2018, the company operates 28,218 locations worldwide, becoming the signature of coffee. Starbuscks also sells all kinds of coffee and desert, their best seller is their Vanilla Latte
